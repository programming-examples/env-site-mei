About our company:

  1. Who we are?
    2. Our company was founded in 1999. We aim to educate people about how they can make a difference. Our company was founded as we strongly believe that, education is the way to make people change. This has been very successful throughout the past couple of years. Everyday we get more and more applicants, looking to help us make a difference.

  2. History
    3. Our team has worked throughout the years by raising awareness to our local community. We visit local schools yearly to educate the children on fun ways to help our planet. We believe children are our future and our hope in creating a greener society. We also visit our local community homes and educated people on how they can make a difference. We investigated easy fun ways to reduce, recuse and recycle. We decided that small steps can create an even bigger step.

  3. Our aims
    4. Our aim is to create a greener environment we do this through education and competitions.
